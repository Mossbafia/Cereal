How To Make

Cereal

Ingredients Needed

    Any Cereal Of Your Choosing

    Milk
    And Most Importantly
    A Bowl
Directions:
  1. Add your desired amount of cereal to the bowl, note not to overfill
    2. Note: if you wish to be careful of nutritional value, check box for serving size.
  2. Next add your milk to the bowl. The amount needed is based simply off of personal preference, however just above half-way is a sure-fire way to make for a good breakfast
    3. Note: any type of milk will suffice
  3. Once desired amount of milk is combined, serve with a spoon, and enjoy!
